# Housing Instability Policy Brief

Policy brief on the compounding effects of housing instability on educational attainment and community economic health.

## Project Outline

This repository contains collaborative work on a policy brief addressing the intersection of housing instability, educational outcomes, and community economic health.

### Repository Structure
- `data/`: Raw and processed datasets related to housing affordability, student performance, and local economic indicators.
- `analysis/`: Scripts and notebooks for statistical analysis and visualization.
- `report-drafts/`: Draft sections of the policy brief in Markdown format.

### Key Sections
1. Impact of Housing Instability on Student Performance
2. Effects on Educational Attainment and Long-term Outcomes
3. Impact on Local Business Vitality and Property Tax Revenue
4. Policy Recommendations
